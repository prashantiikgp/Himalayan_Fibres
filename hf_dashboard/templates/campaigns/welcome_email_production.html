What We Offer

Himalayan Stinging Nettle

A wild Himalayan plant (1200–2000m) naturally grown without pesticides or fertilizers. Sustainably harvested and spun into 100% or blended yarns — popular across Europe and the US.

Himalayan Hemp

Sourced from wild Cannabis Sativa in the Himalayas. Durable, breathable, and eco-friendly — ideal for sustainable textile collections.

Tibetan Wool

Authentic wool from high-altitude regions. Soft, strong, and perfect for warm, luxurious blends.
